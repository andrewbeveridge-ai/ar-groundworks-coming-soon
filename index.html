Where do you work?

Columbus and the surrounding suburbs. Not sure if you're in range? Call or text (380) 261-2194 — we'll confirm in a minute.

How does pricing work?

Every job gets a free, quote. The price you're quoted is all-in with tax included — no hidden fees, no surprise charges at the end.

Why is lawn care a waitlist instead of just bookable?

We build mowing routes neighborhood by neighborhood so every customer gets dependable, same-day service. Once enough neighbors near you join, your route goes live and you're on the list for your neighborhood.

Arch City Property Care is a local owner-operated company, not a call center or a middleman. Whoever is on your property will work to the same high quality standard, and the owner stands behind every visit.

How do I pay?

Zelle or cash is preferred (both save the processing fee and help us keep pricing fair). We also take check or card.
