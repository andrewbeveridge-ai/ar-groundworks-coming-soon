How Routes Go Live

Progress to Launch, Not a Countdown

There's no ticking clock and no false scarcity here. Routes grow as neighbors join — here's how it works.

1

Join the waitlist

Add your name and ZIP. That's it — no commitment, no card.

2

Neighbors add up

We group sign-ups by neighborhood so routes are built efficiently, street by street.

3

Your route nears launch

You'll see this once your route is close — a simple progress note will appear here for your area when it's nearly ready.

4

You're on the route

When it goes live, we start regular mowing in your neighborhood and reach out to schedule.

Why a waitlist instead of just booking? Routes can grow in any neighborhood at any time. Building them deliberately is how we keep mowing dependable and consistent — rather than spreading one owner thin across the whole city.
