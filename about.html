About

Same Guy. Every Visit.

Arch City Property Care is owner-operated — no revolving crew, no call center, no middleman between you and the person doing the work.

When you hire Arch City Property Care, you get a direct line to the owner. The person who answers the phone is the person who shows up, gives you the estimate, and does the work. Same guy every visit.

Andrew started Arch City Property Care to do property work the way it should be done — show up when you say you will, do the job properly, and stand behind it. No revolving crew rotating through your property. No surprises on the invoice. Just honest work, priced to the job.

The work covers the full range of hauling and cleanup across Columbus and the surrounding suburbs: material delivery and spreading, debris removal, junk hauling, brush clearing, leaf removal, storm cleanup, construction site cleanup, move-in/move-out cleanup, and gutter cleaning.

Owner-operated isn't a limitation — it's the whole point. You get accountability, a direct relationship, and someone who treats your property like it matters. Because it does.
